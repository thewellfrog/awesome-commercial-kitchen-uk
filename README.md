# Awesome Ghost Kitchen UK [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of resources for starting and running a ghost kitchen, dark kitchen, or cloud kitchen business in the United Kingdom.

Ghost kitchens (also called dark kitchens, cloud kitchens, or virtual kitchens) are delivery-only food preparation facilities with no customer-facing dining area. The UK market has grown to over 5,500 operations nationwide, driven by platforms like Deliveroo, Uber Eats, and Just Eat.

Whether you're launching your first delivery brand or scaling an existing operation, this list covers everything from finding kitchen space to food safety compliance.

## Finding Kitchen Space

- [KitchenSpaceRentals](https://kitchenspacerentals.com) - UK commercial kitchen directory with 1,300+ verified kitchens across 87 cities. Includes cost calculator, city guides, and operator reviews. Covers ghost kitchens, dark kitchens, and shared kitchen spaces from £15/hour.
- [Peerspace](https://www.peerspace.com/venues/commercial-kitchen) - Marketplace for hourly kitchen rentals, good for pop-ups and recipe testing.
- [Tagvenue](https://www.tagvenue.com/hire/commercial-kitchen) - Event and venue platform with commercial kitchen listings across the UK.
- [We Are Pop Up](https://www.wearepopup.com/) - Platform connecting food businesses with available retail and kitchen spaces.

## Major Kitchen Operators

### National Operators

- [CloudKitchens (FoodStars)](https://www.cloudkitchens.com/) - Uber co-founder Travis Kalanick's ghost kitchen company. 100+ fully-equipped pods across London with integrated delivery management.
- [Karma Kitchen](https://karmakitchen.co/) - Sustainable ghost kitchen operator with sites in London, Manchester, and Birmingham. Known for eco-friendly practices including solar panels and waste reduction.
- [Deliveroo Editions](https://restaurants.deliveroo.com/en-gb/) - Deliveroo's own delivery-only kitchen sites. No rent charged but requires platform exclusivity. 9 London locations plus Manchester and Leeds.
- [Kitchen United](https://www.kitchenunited.com/) - US-based operator expanding in UK. Shared kitchen model allowing multiple brands per facility.

### London-Focused

- [Nimbus Kitchen](https://nimbuskitchen.co.uk/) - Flexible terms and rapid setup. Specialises in helping restaurants launch delivery-only brands.
- [Jacuna Kitchens](https://www.jacunakitchens.com/) - Sites across London, Birmingham, Leeds, and Manchester. Offers exclusive partnerships with major delivery platforms.
- [Mission Kitchen](https://missionkitchen.org/) - Social enterprise supporting food entrepreneurs. Provides mentorship alongside kitchen space.
- [Dephna](https://dephna.com/) - Dark kitchen operator focused on helping established brands expand delivery reach.

## Food Safety & Licensing

### Official Regulatory Bodies

- [Food Standards Agency (FSA)](https://www.food.gov.uk/) - UK government body responsible for food safety. Free online training courses and business registration.
- [FSA Food Business Registration](https://www.food.gov.uk/business-guidance/register-a-food-business) - Register your food business at least 28 days before trading. Required for all food businesses.
- [Food Hygiene Rating Scheme](https://www.food.gov.uk/safety-hygiene/food-hygiene-rating-scheme) - Official ratings database. 64% of customers consider cleanliness when choosing where to eat.

### Industry Support

- [NCASS (Nationwide Caterers Association)](https://www.ncass.org.uk/) - Membership organisation for caterers. Provides Safety Management System, training, and compliance support.
- [NCASS Food Hygiene Guide](https://www.ncass.org.uk/resources/legal-compliance/fhrs-hub/) - Comprehensive guide to achieving a 5-star food hygiene rating.
- [NCASS Safety Management System](https://www.ncass.org.uk/resources/legal-compliance/food-safety-management-system/) - HACCP-compliant documentation pack. Primary Authority approved.

### Training

- [FSA Free Online Courses](https://www.food.gov.uk/business-guidance/food-safety-for-food-businesses) - Free allergen management and food safety training.
- [Level 2 Food Hygiene](https://www.highspeedtraining.co.uk/food-hygiene/level-2-food-hygiene-and-safety-for-catering.aspx) - Essential certification for food handlers. Required before your first EHO inspection.

## Business Funding

### Government-Backed

- [Start Up Loans](https://www.startuploans.co.uk/) - Government-backed loans up to £25,000 at 6% fixed interest. Includes 12 months free mentoring. 100,000+ businesses funded.
- [GOV.UK Start Up Loans](https://www.gov.uk/apply-start-up-loan) - Official application portal. No fees, 1-5 year repayment terms.
- [Virgin StartUp](https://www.virginstartup.org/start-up-loans/) - Start Up Loans delivery partner with dedicated business advisors.
- [The Prince's Trust](https://www.princes-trust.org.uk/help-for-young-people/support-starting-business) - Funding and mentoring for entrepreneurs aged 18-30.

### Alternative Finance

- [British Business Bank](https://www.british-business-bank.co.uk/) - Government development bank offering various SME finance programmes.
- [Funding Circle](https://www.fundingcircle.com/uk/) - Business loans from £10,000-£750,000 for established businesses (1+ year trading).
