# Awesome Commercial Kitchen UK [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of resources for starting and running a ghost kitchen, dark kitchen, or cloud kitchen business in the United Kingdom.

Ghost kitchens (also called dark kitchens, cloud kitchens, or virtual kitchens) are delivery-only food preparation facilities with no customer-facing dining area. The UK market has grown to over 5,500 operations nationwide, driven by platforms like Deliveroo, Uber Eats, and Just Eat.

Whether you're launching your first delivery brand or scaling an existing operation, this list covers everything from finding kitchen space to food safety compliance.

- [Jacuna Kitchens](https://www.jacunakitchens.com/) - Sites across London, Birmingham, Leeds, and Manchester. Offers exclusive partnerships with major delivery platforms.
- [Mission Kitchen](https://missionkitchen.org/) - Social enterprise supporting food entrepreneurs. Provides mentorship alongside kitchen space.
- [Dephna](https://dephna.com/) - Dark kitchen operator focused on helping established brands expand delivery reach.

## Food Safety & Licensing

### Official Regulatory Bodies

- [Food Standards Agency (FSA)](https://www.food.gov.uk/) - UK government body responsible for food safety. Free online training courses and business registration.
- [FSA Food Business Registration](https://www.food.gov.uk/business-guidance/register-a-food-business) - Register your food business at least 28 days before trading. Required for all food businesses.
- [Food Hygiene Rating Scheme](https://www.food.gov.uk/safety-hygiene/food-hygiene-rating-scheme) - Official ratings database. 64% of customers consider cleanliness when choosing where to eat.

### Industry Support

- [NCASS (Nationwide Caterers Association)](https://www.ncass.org.uk/) - Membership organisation for caterers. Provides Safety Management System, training, and compliance support.
- [NCASS Food Hygiene Guide](https://www.ncass.org.uk/resources/legal-compliance/fhrs-hub/) - Comprehensive guide to achieving a 5-star food hygiene rating.
- [NCASS Safety Management System](https://www.ncass.org.uk/resources/legal-compliance/food-safety-management-system/) - HACCP-compliant documentation pack. Primary Authority approved.

### Training

- [FSA Free Online Courses](https://www.food.gov.uk/business-guidance/food-safety-for-food-businesses) - Free allergen management and food safety training.
- [Level 2 Food Hygiene](https://www.highspeedtraining.co.uk/food-hygiene/level-2-food-hygiene-and-safety-for-catering.aspx) - Essential certification for food handlers. Required before your first EHO inspection.
